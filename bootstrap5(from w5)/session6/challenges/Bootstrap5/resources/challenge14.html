Akai

A clumsy panda from a far away Eastern village dreamt every night of becoming a mighty warrior. Akai was fat and clumsy even for panda standards and most people said that all he's good for was rolling round. Howeverthis never fazed Akaias he trained dailyhoping to one day achieve his dreams of becoming a respected warrior. His friends mocked himhis parents discouraged him but Akai persisted. Soon one daya mysterious old monk noticed Akai's persistence and took him under his guidance. Akai flourished under the training of the monkmastering the arts of the flail. He is now ready to get onto the world stage and prove that he is a worthy member of the martial arts world!
